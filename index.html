I'm Glad You're Here.

My practice at a glance

Being human in this world can be tough. My goal as a therapist is to sit with my clients in these difficult times.
I provide my clients a relaxed, comfortable, and trusting environment where they can feel heard, seen, and valued.
I see it as a great privilege to partner with my clients as they share and explore their stories and experiences.
Together, we can navigate what can often feel like an overwhelming world.
I utilize an integrative approach that is collaborative and relationship-based, strengths-focused, with strong emphasis
on Cognitive Behavioral and Client-Centered therapies. I address issues including death of a loved one, living
with / supporting someone who is living with terminal illness, life transitions, anxiety. I have experience working with
adults, adolescents and children.
I believe that compassion, humor, and collaboration help to create a comfortable counseling space for my clients.
