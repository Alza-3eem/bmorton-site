Services

My practice offers a complimentary 15-minute phone consultation.
Afterward, my fee for an initial consultation is $150 then each individual therapy session is $125 per 50-minute session.
I accept cash, checks, major credit cards, and FSA/HSA cards.
I am in-network with most BCBS plans (not BlueHome) and UHC/Optum (not UMR), Cigna, Aetna.
I can provide invoices ready for you to file out-of-network insurance claims as well.
I am not an ACA, Medicare, or Medicaid provider at this time.
Telehealth only.
